My take

3:2 or 4:3 is the sweet spot for this collection. GVC characters are tall portraits, scene prompts are landscapes — both tolerate a near-square box without crazy letterbox on either type.

Go 16:10 or 16:9 if you plan to lean into landscape/scene outputs as the headline prompts. Portraits will have visible side padding but stay readable.

Avoid 2:1 unless you're designing for very wide hero images specifically — character renders get squeezed into a tiny center band and look lost.
